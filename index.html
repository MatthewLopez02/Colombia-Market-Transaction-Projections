⚠️ Risks & 🚀 Opportunities

Risk Factors That Could Reduce Transaction Frequency

⚠️ Competitive Pressure

Remitly, Wise launch aggressive promotions or price wars

Mitigation: Lock in users early with superior product experience; build brand loyalty through community engagement

⚠️ Economic Downturn

US recession reduces remittance volumes overall

Mitigation: Diversify into domestic Colombia products (savings, payments); position as essential service

⚠️ COP Volatility

Unfavorable exchange rates discourage transfers

Mitigation: Dynamic pricing, rate alerts, forward contract options; educate users on long-term averaging

⚠️ Regulatory Issues

Compliance challenges in Colombia slow growth

Mitigation: Strong legal/compliance team in place pre-launch; conservative rollout strategy; local partnerships

Upside Scenarios That Could Increase Frequency

🚀 Network Effects

Users invite family/friends through referral program - viral growth

Impact: Could increase user base to 50,000+ and frequency to 6.5+ transactions/month

🚀 Product Expansion

Add bill pay, mobile top-ups, savings accounts - more reasons to use app

Impact: Increases stickiness; users return more frequently; transaction frequency +20-30%

🚀 Competitor Weakness

Western Union continues losing market share to digital-first platforms

Impact: Larger addressable market; easier to acquire users; could exceed 35K target by 30%

🚀 Cultural Events

Christmas, Mother's Day, Colombian holidays spike usage significantly

Impact: Seasonal spikes to 8-10 transactions/month; opportunity for targeted campaigns

🚀 Gig Economy Growth

More Colombians immigrating to US for work = more remittances

Impact: Growing market; early positioning as preferred platform; TAM expansion

🚀 B2B Partnerships

Partner with employers hiring Colombian workers (construction, hospitality)

Impact: Direct deposit + remittance bundling; higher engagement; enterprise deals
